FAQs

  • How do I build ‘My job Finder’ profile?

    A. If you are a new user, you will be asked to enter in your preferred e-mail address and create a password. Please note your user name and password for future use. Complete the fields to create your profile on JobConnect. You will be asked for personal information such as address. You will then select the type of job functions and locations that you are interested in. Based on the job functions that you have selected you will be asked to give more detail about your skills and experience. We highly recommend that a resume is included in your profile so that we may fully evaluate all of your experience and education. If you find that you are receiving too few or too many job notifications, you may want to adjust your profile preferences. To receive more notifications, try broadening your preferences by simply selecting those for location and job category/function, rather than organization. To receive fewer notifications, narrow your choices and be sure to include job function(s) and location city for the narrowest possible return. To provide the best possible match, we encourage you to update your profile regularly. If you do not wish to receive any further job notifications, simply deselect the check box labeled "Please e-mail me job opportunities that match my profile" in My Job Finder. Auto-match is based on your Job Category, Location, and Skill Assessment selections.

  • How do I apply for a job?

    A. From the search results list, click on the Job Title link to read more about the job. If you are interested, click the "Apply Now" button. From here you will be asked to answer a series of questions, complete a skills assessment, and enter your resume if you have not already.

  • What is my Personal Job List?

    A. Once you have completed your profile, you can click the "Show Jobs Matching My Profile" link which will show you a list of current openings which match your profile. Any job that displays the "Pencil" icon next to the job title indicates that you have already applied to this job.

  • Is my job profile confidential?

    A. Yes, the information you provide is confidential, and will only be viewed by our recruiters in an effort to fill open positions for which you may be qualified. In addition the information you provide is subject to our privacy policy practices.

  • What pre-employment processes will I need to complete if I receive an employment offer?

    A. In the event that you are pre-selected for a position, there are a number of pre-employment processes you'll need to successfully complete in order to start work. They include various background checks, fingerprint processing and drug screening, as well as several onboarding forms. For certain roles, there may be additional pre-employment screening criteria required. We'll keep you updated about all the steps you need to take throughout the pre-employment process.
